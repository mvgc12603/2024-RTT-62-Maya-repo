    Victoria Chelle

    Studied art for 4 years at the National School of Arts in Montevideo, Uruguay.

    Studied with Uruguayan artist Anhelo Hernandez in Montevideo.

    Moved to Washington, D.C., where I studied with Roberto Morassi.

    In 2001, I attended a private extended workshop with Alceu Ribeiro at his studio in Palma de Mallorca, Spain.


    Was in charge of the Inter-American Development Bank's Painting Workshop, organizing their exhibitions for over 10 years.
    Member of the Art Committee of the Inter-American Development Bank Staff Association's Art Gallery. In charge of selecting the artwork to be exhibited each year at the gallery.


    Participated in group exhibitions at the Organization of American States, the Pan American Health Organization, the Inter-American Development Bank, the Embassy of Argentina, the Embassy of Haiti, the Embassy of Uruguay, the Martin Luther King Library, and various restaurants in the DC area.
